📘 MODULE 5: Simulation & Scenario Lab
1️⃣ Purpose
To allow users to test:
“What happens if…”
Without real disruption occurring.
This is your strategic planning module.
2️⃣ Target Users
Supply Chain Director


Risk Officer


Executive leadership
3️⃣ Core Capabilities
A. What-If Simulation
User can simulate:
Supplier shutdown


Port congestion increase


Country trade ban


Demand spike


Plant capacity drop


System then:
Recalculates risk


Shows impacted nodes


Estimates lead time increase


Estimates cost increase


B. Cascading Impact Visualization
If Tier-2 fails:
Highlight:
Tier-1 impact


Plant impact


SKU impact


Warehouse impact


Graph dynamically updates.

Instead of just showing alerts, simulate:
“What happens if this disruption lasts 10 days?”
Example:
Event: Port congestion
Duration: 12 days
System calculates:
Shipment delay: +9 days
Inventory coverage: 7 days
Result: Plant shutdown risk
Revenue impact: $2.3M

C. Financial Impact Estimation (Simplified)
User inputs:
Revenue per SKU per day


System estimates:
 Revenue Loss = Days of disruption × daily revenue
This makes it business-relevant.
D. Branch Comparison
User selects:
Current route


Alternative route


System compares:
Risk under disruption


Cost difference


Delay impact


Graphical comparison chart.
4️⃣ Example Scenario
“What if Taiwan API stops for 30 days?”
System shows:
Affected plants


SKUs impacted


Inventory coverage days


Revenue at risk


Suggested mitigation
5️⃣ Success Criteria
User can simulate failure in 1 click


Graph updates dynamically


Financial impact visible


Comparison view intuitive
